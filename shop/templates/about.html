Who are we?

At Circuit Bunny, we're not just a store; we're a community dedicated to fueling your passion for electronics and robotics. Since our launch on May 11, we've been on a mission to be your go-to destination for all things electrical and robotic. Our journey began with a commitment to providing high-quality components and exceptional service, and now, with over 100 satisfied customers and 5000 components sold, we're ready to take the next step.

Our Philosophy

Our philosophy is simple: to make learning and innovation accessible to everyone. With a comprehensive range of products, from essential breadboards to cutting-edge Arduinos, we've curated a collection that caters to both novices and seasoned enthusiasts alike.

Our Values

Our values include a commitment to offering competitive prices, ensuring fast shipping, and providing top-notch customer service. We understand that having the right components is crucial to the success of creative projects, and that's why we've meticulously selected products that are not only reliable but also foster creativity and exploration.

What should our Website Reflect?

Already having delivered to over 100 individuals, we've witnessed the power of Circuit Bunny components bringing ideas to life. The success of our startup is a testament to the dedication of our team of experts who are as passionate about circuits as you are. As we transition to our online platform, we want our website to reflect the vibrant community that Circuit Bunny has become.
